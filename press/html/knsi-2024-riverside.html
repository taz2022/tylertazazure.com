Rock the Riverside Returns to The Clearing Thursday

Saint Cloud, MN, USA / KNSI
Jennifer Lewerenz
Jul 11, 2024 | 12:13 PM

(KNSI) — Sauk Rapids Rock the Riverside returns Thursday evening.

Tonight, it’s Raised on Radio with special guests Taz and T-Bone.

Raised on Radio is made up of several musicians and vocalists from well known central Minnesota bands such as Diamondback, Outside Recess, Pandemic and Chaser. The group performs several different shows that cover a wide range of music, from Foreigner and Night Ranger to Alabama and Bon Jovi.

The opening acts start at 5:30, and the headliner starts at 7:00. Everything wraps at 9:30. The shows are set for every Thursday evening at The Clearing all summer. They are free and open to everyone.

Last week’s show was postponed due to threatening weather. Radio Nation and special guest False Autumn will play a makeup date on September 12th.
