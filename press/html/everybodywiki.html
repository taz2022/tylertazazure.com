Code Red Riot is an American hard rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada. They were signed to Sony RED Music in 2017 after posting an early demo of “Living Low” on Facebook, and receiving radio airplay on Sirius/XM Octane.[1] They have released one full-length album titled Mask (2018),[2] and one music video for their song "Living Low."[3] Their frontman and songwriter, Corky Gainsford, was formerly the drummer for the band Otherwise on Century Media Records, but left that band in 2015 to start Code Red Riot.[4] On Mask, Gainsford wrote all the songs and played all the primary instruments (drums, bass, rhythm guitar and vocals), while the lead guitar duties were handled by Tyler "Taz" Azure.[5] The album was co-produced by Gainsford and Shawn McGhee[1] (Disturbed, Five Finger Death Punch, Hellyeah, Papa Roach).[6]
